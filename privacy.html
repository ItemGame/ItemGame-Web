Privacy Policy for Item Game

Effective Date: June 17th, 2025

Item Game Developers LLC ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of Item Game (the “Game”).

1. Information We Do Not Collect

At this stage of development, Item Game does not collect any personal information from players. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Gameplay analytics or telemetry
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

2. Optional Data You May Provide

If you contact us (e.g., by emailing averyfd@shaw.ca to report a bug or provide feedback), we may receive any information you include in that communication, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Details about your experience with the Game

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and improve the Game.

3. Crash Reports and Debug Data (If Enabled)

If crash reporting or debug logging is enabled, the Game may generate logs that include:

  • System information (e.g., operating system, GPU, memory status)
  • Game state or error details at the time of the crash

These logs are used only for internal testing and development and are not linked to any identifiable individual.

4. No Sharing or Selling of Data

We do not share, sell, or rent any player information to third parties. All development data is used exclusively by Item Game Developers LLC for improving the Game.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided to us, we will delete it promptly.

6. Security

Although we do not actively collect personal data, we follow standard industry practices to protect any data that may be submitted voluntarily (e.g., via feedback emails) against unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the Game or to comply with legal requirements. When updates are made, we will notify users through:

  • An in-game notice
  • Email notification (if contact has been provided)

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: averyfd@shaw.ca
